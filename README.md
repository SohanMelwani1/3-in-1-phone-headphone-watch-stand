# 3-in-1-phone-headphone-watch-stand
A phone stand + headphone holder + watch holder

This was designed to neatly store your phone, headphones and watch while organizing your table

While making it, I tried featuring a minamalist design while making a small organizer which takes much lesser space compared to others

This organizer is very costumizable as the headphone and watch holders are printed separatley from the phone holder, so you can costumize those 2 according to your preference.


Instructions:

Print using PLA black(color is up to you)

Remove supports and sand down un even parts

glue the headphone stand and watch stand in place, in their own slots

Adjust the phone sliders(there are 2 slots)


Print settings:

Printed with Bambu Labs A1 mini, PLA Black

Enable supports

Infill 8%
